This document contains specific visual descriptors to ensure AI-generated images are culturally and geographically accurate for the Summit Chronicles blog.

## Mani Stones
**Keywords:** mani stone, prayer stone, carbed mantra, om mani padme hum
**Visual Description:** Piles or walls of irregular river stones or slate rocks. Each stone is carved with the Tibetan mantra "Om Mani Padme Hum" in distinct Tibetan script. The carvings are often painted in white, red, green, yellow, and blue. Found along hiking paths, chortens, and mountain passes. Texture is mossy, weathered, and rough. Often accompanied by colorful prayer flags fluttering in the wind.
**Prompt Modifiers:** close up of carved stone tablet, tibetan mantra inscription, weathered slate, mossy texture, colorful painted letters, stacked stones, religious artifact, nepalese style.

## Sherpa People
**Keywords:** sherpa, guide, porter, climber features
**Visual Description:** Indigenous people of the Himalayas. Distinctive facial features: high cheekbones, bronzed/weathered skin from sun and wind at altitude, dark eyes, jet black hair (often worn short or under hats). Traditional clothing might include thick wool bakhus (robes) or modern technical climbing gear mixed with yak wool hats. Warm, resilient expressions. PLEASE NOTE: Avoid generic "asian" AI faces; look for specific Tibetan/Sherpa ethnicity traits.
**Prompt Modifiers:** sherpa ethnicity, weathered skin, high cheekbones, tibetan facial features, authentic mountaineering clothing, warm expression, himalayan native, detailed cultural portrait.

## Stupas / Chortens
**Keywords:** stupa, chorten, buddhist shrine
**Visual Description:** A dome-shaped structure representing the Buddha's mind. Whitewashed dome (kumbha), square base, golden spire (harmika) on top with the "eyes of the Buddha" painted on all four sides. Often adorned with streamers of prayer flags connecting the spire to the ground. specific feature: The "Eyes" are stylized, blue or black, looking out serenely.
**Prompt Modifiers:** buddhist stupa, whitewashed dome, golden spire, eyes of buddha painted on tower, prayer flags radiating, himalayan architecture, spiritual landmark.

## Himalayan Landscape (Ladakh / Nepal)
**Keywords:** landscape, mountains, himalayas, scenery
**Visual Description:** Dramatic, jagged peaks covered in snow and ice. Deep valleys with rocky, arid terrain (Ladakh style) or lush pine forests (lower Nepal). The light is harsh and contrasty (high altitude). Sky is deep, polarized blue.
**Style:** National Geographic photography, dramatic lighting, golden hour, wide angle, epic scale.
**Prompt Modifiers:** jagged snow capped peaks, deep blue sky, high altitude terrain, arid rocky valley, dramatic shadows, atmospheric depth, 8k resolution.

## Prayer Flags
**Keywords:** prayer flags, lung ta
**Visual Description:** Strings of small rectangular cloth flags in five specific colors: Blue, White, Red, Green, Yellow. They are usually strung diagonally from high points (stupas, peaks) to low points. They look weathered, frayed edges from the wind.
**Prompt Modifiers:** colorful prayer flags, fluttering in wind, five colors (blue white red green yellow), frayed edges, weathered cloth, festive spiritual atmosphere.
